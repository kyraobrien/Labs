Kyra's 2023 Fall Must Haves

Fashion Accessories Products More

Fall is now in full swing and with a new season comes new trends. From my favorite wardrobe essentials, to my coffee order, here are a few of my must haves this season.

And More!

  • Pumpkin Cream Cold Brew

    • I love any fall drink, but the Starbucks Pumpkin Cream Cold Brew is a go-to every fall. I love that it's not overly sweet. I add a touch of oatmilk for an extra creaminess.

  • Gold+Ivy Candles

    • I'm convinced that Gold+Ivy doesnt have a candle scent that I dont like. They have a few fall scents from "Bourbon Apple Cider" to "Maple Chai". All of the candles are free of toxins and have an amazing burn! I recommend this company all the time!

  • Self-Improvement Book

    • I just recently got the book "101 Essays That Will Change The Way You Think" by Brianna Wiest and so far I've really enjoyed reading this!
