Fall is now in full swing and with a new season comes new trends. From my favorite wardrobe essentials, to my coffee order, here are a few of my must haves this season.

Must Have Accessories

  • Sunglasses

    • Even though summer is coming to an end, the sun is still shining many days of fall! Dont ditch the sunglasses just yet! These sunglasses are so cute and can add some class to an outfit.


  • Leather Fanny Pack

    • I got this fanny pack during the summer and have worn it almost everyday! I will continue to wear this throughout the fall to hold my essentials when I'm on the go. The warm color of the leather is perfect for fall. I got the Woven Remy bag in "Tan".
