My Favorite Recipe

Scotcharoos

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup light corn syrup
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 3 cups Rice Krispies
  • 1 bag of milk chocolate chips
  • 1 bag of butterscotch chips

Instructions

  1. In a large microwave safe bowl, add sugar,corn syrup and butter. Heat for about 3 to 3.5 minutes.
  2. Remove from microwave and then stir in peanut butter until fully combined. Then gradually stir in the Rice Krispies.
  3. Transfer contents of the bowl into a greased 9x13 inch pan.
  4. Press mixture flat into pan using the back of a spoon.
  5. Next, add both bags of chips into a glass bowl. Microwave for 60 seconds. Then microwave in 30 second intervals until chocolate is fully melted.
  6. Spread chocolate onto the bars in the pan
  7. Let bars cool for an hour or until set.
